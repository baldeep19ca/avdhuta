Stress Relief
30-Second Witness Technique
🫁
Tap to Begin
1
Locate
Where is this stress in your body right now? Chest? Throat? Stomach? Simply finding it creates distance from it.
2
Witness
"There is stress happening. And I am the one noticing the stress." The noticer is never stressed. Find the noticer.
3
Return
One slow breath. "I am the sky. This is a cloud passing through." Now act — from that calmer centre.

Weekly Mauna Practice
Once this week: 60 minutes of sacred silence. No phone, no music, no task. Sit with the question: "Who am I, beneath everything?" You don't need to answer. The question itself begins to dissolve the false answers.
"The student asked: What is Brahman? The teacher pointed to the sky and was silent. That was the teaching."
